Introduction

Chi-squared (\(\chi^{2}\)) contingency table

Ladybird morphs are not equally distributed in the two habitats (Chi squared = 19.1028921 , df = 1, P = 1.2385706^{-5}), with black morphs being more frequent in the industrial habitat.

Two-sample t-test.

Gentoo penguins are significantly heavier than Adelie penguins (t-test, t = -23.3860277, df = 249.6425536, P = 7.7098231^{-65}).
